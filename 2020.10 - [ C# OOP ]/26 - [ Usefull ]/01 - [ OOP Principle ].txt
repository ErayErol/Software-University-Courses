ABSTRACTION - Modeling objects that are relevant to the application, the abtraction give us options what we can use

INHERITANCE - Reusing properties and members of a class in order not to repeat and keep the code clean

ENCAPSULATION - To hide data from the outside world. Encapsulation works with access modifiers to protect
the info and objects in the application from changing or bad behaviour

POLYMORPHISM - To reuse an object through one of his base classes
